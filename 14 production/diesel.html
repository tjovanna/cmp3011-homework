Diesel Café

6am-11pm, M-Su
257 Elm Street

Past the counter and toward the pool table, Diesel Café has 2 single stall bathrooms. It seems like these bathrooms get a fair amount of traffic. While they aren’t the cleanest in town, they’re also not disgusting. Staff doesn’t bat an eye if you waltz past the counter, and you don’t need a key to get in.
