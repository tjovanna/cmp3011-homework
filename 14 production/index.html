URGENT BUSINESS


  • Diesel Café

    257 Elm Street
    6am-11pm, M-Su

    Past the counter and toward the pool table, Diesel Café has 2 single stall bathrooms. It seems like these bathrooms get a fair amount of traffic. While they aren’t the cleanest in town, they’re also not disgusting. Staff doesn’t bat an eye if you waltz past the counter, and you don’t need a key to get in.

  • iYo Bistro

    234 Elm Street
    9am-11pm, M-Su

    iYo Bistro was recently remodeled and its bathrooms are cleaner than ever. It isn’t too busy (with most people hanging out to do work), but the self-serve fro-yo is in the back near the bathrooms and no one will suspect a thing if the first thing you do is make a beeline for the back.

  • Starbucks

    260 Elm Street
    5:30am-10:30pm, M-Su

    Starbucks isn’t known for having the cleanest or freest bathrooms, and this one is no exception. You may have to buy a coffee for the code just to wade through toilet paper strewn about the floor, but you won’t find a place open earlier.

  • Flatbread Company

    45 Day Street
    11:30am-10pm, M-Su

    Flatbread Company does a pretty good job of keeping their bathrooms clean. If you have a large group of people in need of some relief, the multiple-stalled bathrooms make this the spot for you. The friendly staff are happy to point you to the bathroom most days. If anyone gives you a hard time, you can always say you’re there for bowling.

  • Five Horse Tavern

    400 Highland Avenue
    11am-1am, M-Su

    By day a family restaurant, by night a bustling sports bar: the ease of access to Five Horses largely depends on the time of day. At night, you’ll have no problem slipping in for free if you’re over 21, but you may have to shove your way through a crowd. During lunch, it’s going to be difficult to get access without asking for a table. (Our tip? Ask for a seat at the bar.)

  • Rosebud American Kitchen & Bar

    381 Summer Street
    11:30am-12am, M-Su

    This one is super easy, and the bathrooms are pretty nice. There’s usually a lot going on at the Rosebud, so all you have to do is confidently walk back to the bathrooms and no one will question you. They’re too busy.
